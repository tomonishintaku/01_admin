Statement

I create art works that focus on the concept of food, based on my experience of studying at a culinary school and obtaining a cooking license following my graduation from a university of the arts. I experiment with familiar, everyday food items like instant noodles and cake to critically portray modern life and social issues in a plain, simple, and cynical fashion. (23 May, 2018)
